Treatments

Our treatments.

Choose from traditional Thai massage, focused deep tissue work, relaxing aromatherapy treatments and shorter sessions for everyday tension.

01

Traditional Thai Massage

The classic full-body treatment, using rhythmic pressure and gentle stretching to release tension, improve flexibility and leave you deeply relaxed. Performed clothed on a comfortable mat.

02

Deep Tissue Massage

Focused, firmer work for tight, knotted muscles. Ideal if you carry stress in your shoulders and back, or train hard and need proper recovery.

03

Aromatherapy Massage

A slower, soothing treatment with warm essential oils, chosen to calm the mind as well as the body. Our most relaxing option.

04

Head, Neck & Shoulder Massage

A targeted treatment for desk-based tension, headaches and tight shoulders. Perfect as a shorter session on a busy day.

05

Couples Massage

Side-by-side treatments in our double room — a calm hour to unwind together. Popular for birthdays, anniversaries and thoughtful gifts.

06

Thai Herbal Body Scrub

A gentle exfoliating treatment that leaves skin soft and refreshed. A lovely add-on before or after massage.
